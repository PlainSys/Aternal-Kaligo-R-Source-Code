Saweba - The First Mission readme.txt

 Thank you for downloading and playing Saweba 1. This will be one of my last games for Godot
before I switch to a different engine. This was also one of longest games I have made and the game that took
the longest to make (I wonder why >_>). Anyways, if you for whatever reason you make a video on this, it would
be epik if you credit us (Aranorten) and leave the download link so others can play (for some reason...)

If you want more information, you can check out the manual txt... or if your one of the skids who actually has
this physically on a disc, check the mini manual behind the cool cover art.

For controls, use the arrow keys to move around and use the z key to attack! Those are the controls.

Also, if you get a high score, you should totally join the Aranorten discord server and share it.
