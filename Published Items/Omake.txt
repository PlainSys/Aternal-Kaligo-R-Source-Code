■０．Introduction
  Saweba is a top down shooting game that was inspired by games
like Berzerk, Doom, Touhou, Undertale, Robotron, and other arcade
hits. The goal of each stage is to get to the end, with the exception
of some stages such as Binersa's stage 1/6 and Remed's stage 1.
Every stage lies a boss too, waiting to fight and kill you.

■1．Characters
  Binersa (and Yellow) is best for long range combat while Remed is
best for close range. Binersa is slow while Remed is fast. The two
are based on the old characters of Shooterbot and Bunny from the
PolyHeck days! They now have official names and some backstory...
kinda.

■2．Canonicity
  Saweba is an interesting game since, despite its joking aspects,
is the first game in the PolyHeck series to have an actual story.
This begs the question, what is the timeline of this franchise?
Is Shooterbot vs Rasberry canon? Didnt PolyHeck die in PolyHeck 2?
Why is he in PolyHeck 3 if he's dead??? In my opinion, this is how
the official timeline goes

PolyHeck
Saweba
PolyHeck 2

PolyHeck 3, Shooterbot vs Rasberry, and Albadil are NOT canon to the
timeline. Albadil takes place in an alternate universe so if anything,
its following its own timeline (new Albadil game maybe??? :0)

Anyways, Due to PolyHeck 2 taking place in 2020, I decided that Saweba
and PolyHeck 2 take place at the same time! So while Binersa is
fighting for his life, Rasberry is running for his life.

■3．Endings
  Automatically, your locked into the bad ending if you play on easy or
beat the game without continues. To get the true ending, you must beat
the game without losing continues which is very tough so good luck >:)

Also, there is a secret route and secret ending for both Binersa and Remed
which involve finding some black orbs. Good luck finding my secrets!
