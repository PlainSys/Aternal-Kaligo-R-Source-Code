	âœªMain Storyâœª

After the events of PolyHeck, Binersa and Remed got hired by an assassination company known as Gapila Corp.
Their mission was to hunt down Bumathule in order to retrieve an artifact known as the Dream Box. Remed
was sent first before Binersa and his new pal Yellow were sent. The trio must traverse through Bumathule's
facility in order to find and kill him. Along the way however, the three will face off against new foes
and meet some old foes?!

Binersa: Binersa aka Shooterbot is the main character. He has an arm cannon and can shoot from afar, but is
very slow when compared to his friend.

having to rely on his spear to attack.

	âœªGameplayâœª

  The goal of the game is to beat every stage to progress to stage 6 and beat Bumathule. Most stages have
maze like areas you must navigate to fight the boss. Buttons are also a thing which you must shoot/slash to
progress. Some stages have timers though, where you have to wait for a certain time to beat the stage.
Anyways, goodluck dodging and beating every stage!
